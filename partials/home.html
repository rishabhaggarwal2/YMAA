Why should you care?

Of the top 10 leading causes of death, Alzheimer’s is the 6th and the only one that cannot be slowed, prevented or cured.

More than 5 million people are living with Alzheimer's Disease in the U.S. This number is predicted to reach up to 16 million by 2050.

Nearly two thirds of Americans with Alzheimers are women.

Where are we now?

Since we started in 2011, we have come a long way with over 20 chapters across the nation and over 15 partnering companies that care about the cause as much as we do and are doing our best to spread awareness and push for medical research in the field.
